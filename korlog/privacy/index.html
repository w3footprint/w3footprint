Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

KörLog is developed and operated by W3Footprint enskild firma, Sweden. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

What data we collect

  • Account data — your email address when you create an account
  • Session data — driving sessions you log, including start and end times, duration, break time, earnings, distance, platform, and any notes you add

We do not collect location data, device identifiers, or any data you have not explicitly entered.

How your data is stored

Your data is stored locally on your device first. If you are signed in, it is also synced to Firebase Firestore under your account. Only you can access your data — it is scoped to your user ID and protected by Firebase security rules that prevent any other user from reading or writing it.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not share your data with third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising or profiling

Your rights (GDPR)

As a user in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@w3footprint.se. We will respond within 30 days.

Firebase

KörLog uses Google Firebase for authentication and cloud sync. Google's privacy policy applies to data processed through Firebase. See firebase.google.com/support/privacy for details.

Data retention

Your data is kept for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is removed from our systems within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of KörLog after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
