Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

FriLuft is developed and operated by W3Footprint enskild firma, Sweden. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

What data we collect

  • Location data — your device's GPS coordinates, used only to fetch local weather. Location is never stored on our servers or shared with third parties.
  • Last searched city — the name and coordinates of the last city you searched for, stored locally on your device only.

We do not collect your name, email address, account information, or any other personal data.

How your data is stored

All data (weather cache and last city preference) is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to our servers. FriLuft does not have a backend or user accounts.

Weather data

Weather information is fetched from SMHI Open Data, a free public API provided by the Swedish Meteorological and Hydrological Institute. Your coordinates are sent to SMHI's servers to retrieve weather. See SMHI's privacy notice for details on how they handle data.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not share your data with third parties (except SMHI for weather lookups)
  • We do not use your data for advertising or profiling
  • We do not store your location on any server

Your rights (GDPR)

As a user in the EU/EEA, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any data we hold about you. Since FriLuft stores all data locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

For any questions, contact us at hello@w3footprint.se. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of FriLuft after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
